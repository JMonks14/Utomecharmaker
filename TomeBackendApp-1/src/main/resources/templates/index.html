Welcome to The Unfinished Tome


Think you of the fact that a deaf person cannot hear. Then, what deafness may we not all possess? What senses do we lack that we cannot see and cannot hear another world all around us? What is there around us that we cannot know? - Frank Herbert, Dune



The Unfinished Tome is a LARP (Live Action RolePlay) game run by the University of Nottingham Roleplaying and Wargames society. Events take place throughout the academic year on University Park campus, including Saturday evening comba sessions in Highfields park, and Tuesday evening non-combat sessions indoors. This website will allow you to set up a game account which, when logged in, will allow you to create a character and buy skills to play the game.
